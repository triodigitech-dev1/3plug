# administration.md
## 3PlugPOS — Administration Framework

This document defines the **Administration Plug (Administration Engine)** of 3PlugPOS.

It is structured in two phases:

1. Shared Administrative Functions (common across nearly all industries)
2. Category-Specific Administrative Functions (unique governance & compliance needs per industry group)

---

# Summary

The Administration Plug of 3PlugPOS:

- Ensures regulatory compliance
- Maintains governance integrity
- Protects legal standing
- Controls financial administration
- Secures organizational records

Together with Operations and Management, the Administration Engine completes the full 3PlugPOS architecture:

Operations → Execution  
Management → Strategy  
Administration → Control & Compliance  

This ensures 3PlugPOS functions as a complete business operating core.
