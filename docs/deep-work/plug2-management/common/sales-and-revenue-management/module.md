# Sales & Revenue Management

## Module Scope
This module provides controlled execution and traceability for Sales & Revenue Management in Management.

## Module Dashboard
### Primary Widgets
- Throughput/health KPI cards
- Pending action queue
- Exceptions and risk alerts
- Recent activity timeline

### Quick Actions
- Create new workflow item
- Continue draft/in-progress item
- Approve/escalate where permitted
- Export operational/managerial/admin report

### Drilldowns
- By branch/unit
- By user/owner
- By time window
- By category/segment

## Functions
- Sales target setting
- Sales pipeline oversight
- Channel performance analysis
- Customer lifetime value analysis
- Pricing strategy management
- Discount policy governance
- Promotion effectiveness analysis
- Revenue diversification tracking
