# Financial Administration

## Module Scope
This module provides controlled execution and traceability for Financial Administration in Administration.

## Module Dashboard
### Primary Widgets
- Throughput/health KPI cards
- Pending action queue
- Exceptions and risk alerts
- Recent activity timeline

### Quick Actions
- Create new workflow item
- Continue draft/in-progress item
- Approve/escalate where permitted
- Export operational/managerial/admin report

### Drilldowns
- By branch/unit
- By user/owner
- By time window
- By category/segment

## Functions
- General ledger management
- Chart of accounts structuring
- Accounts payable management
- Accounts receivable oversight
- Payroll processing
- Tax calculation & reporting
- VAT / sales tax tracking
- Withholding tax tracking
- Bank reconciliation
- Fixed asset register management
- Depreciation tracking
- Financial statement generation (P&L, Balance Sheet, Cash Flow)
- Audit trail maintenance
- Budget control enforcement
- Expense approval workflows
