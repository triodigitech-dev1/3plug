# Summary

The Business Operations Plug of 3PlugPOS:

- Covers universal operational workflows
- Adapts to industry-specific requirements
- Maintains centralized data integrity
- Enables scalability across sectors
- Integrates seamlessly with Management & Administration plugs

This structure ensures 3PlugPOS can operate as a cross-industry modular business core.
