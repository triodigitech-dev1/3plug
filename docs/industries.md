## Notes (Scope Fit)
3PlugPOS fits best where an organization needs:
- **Transactions + inventory (Operations)**
- **Decision dashboards + KPIs (Management)**
- **Accounting/HR/compliance (Administration)**

This applies to most SMEs and many mid-market organizations across the categories above.
